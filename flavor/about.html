We Live to Entertain

Who We Are

Flavor started out performing in Night Clubs; we’ve traveled all the back roads playing Colleges. From the Southern most point of Mississippi, to the Northern tip of Chapel Hill. We’ve played Beach Clubs, Country Clubs, and everything in-between. Flavor began Turing the world by performing for the troops during the first Gulf War. We’ve toured Japan, Germany, Australia, New Zealand, Portugal, and all across the USA and we pride ourselves on it, because that’s what makes us unique. We’ve taken this experience and put together a repertoire of the best songs, people love to hear, sing and dance to. All that’s left is one of the most exciting and entertaining shows you’ve ever been apart of. You’re guaranteed to have a great time when Flavor’s in the house.

Let's face it...

Your number one concern on that big day is whether the band will get the crowd up and celebrating the whole night. Well for over a decade we’ve been doing just that. There’s no secrete to the magic, because the magic is you. You pick the songs from our song list, we stir up the music, match the tempos, mix the melody’s, and Sprinkle just the right Flavor to create a night you and your guest will never forget. On stage we love to have a good time. Likewise the crowd joins in as well. Apart of this excitement is crowd participation, including the audience can make for an unbelievable night if the music is right… and with our magic together it will be. If the party’s depending on you make sure you’re depending on Flavor The Band.
